# Privacy Policy — Helix Decide

Last updated: July 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to HelixLabs. We operate the website helixlabs.studio and our suite of products including Helix Decide (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Helix Decide on Android. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Email address (when signing in to save decisions)
- Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- App usage data and interaction patterns
- Device type and operating system version
- IP address and general location data

### 2.3 Decision Input Data
When you use Helix Decide, the text, descriptions, or context you enter is sent to our backend server and then to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a decision card. This content is:

- Sent to our Vercel backend solely to process your request
- Forwarded to Anthropic's API to generate the AI response
- Not stored on HelixLabs servers after processing
- Not used to train AI models

Decision cards you save are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage and are never uploaded to our servers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Verify your account and subscription plan
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Provide and improve the App
- Send transactional emails (receipts, account updates)
- Respond to customer support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

- **Stripe** — for payment processing
- **Anthropic** — decision input text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data.
- **Vercel** — for backend hosting
- **Neon** — for database hosting (email and subscription plan only)
- Law enforcement when required by law

## 5. Decision Data

When you enter text into Helix Decide:

- Your input is sent to our backend to call the Claude API
- The AI-generated response is returned to your device
- Neither your input nor the response is stored on our servers
- Decision cards you choose to save are stored locally on your device only
- Clearing the app data or uninstalling the app removes all locally saved decisions

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account data (email and subscription plan) for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Locally stored decision cards can be deleted at any time from within the app or by clearing app data on your device.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@helixlabs.studio

## 8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your authentication token is stored securely using expo-secure-store on your device. However, no method of storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
