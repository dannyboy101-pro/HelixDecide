# Delete Your Helix Decide Account

To request deletion of your Helix Decide account and all associated data, contact us at:

**Email:** support@helixlabs.studio  
**Subject line:** Account Deletion Request

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request and permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

## What gets deleted
- Your email address and account information
- Your subscription and billing records
- Any usage data associated with your account

## Note on local data
Decision cards and history in Helix Decide are stored locally on your device only and are not held on our servers. To remove this data, uninstall the app or clear app data from your Android settings.
